How It Works

From Instagram DMs
to a Real Business.

AFRITEX replaces scattered DM conversations with a structured, professional commerce flow — in three simple steps.

01
🏪

Build Your Storefront

Register, get verified, and build a professional branded storefront with your logo, bio, and full product catalogue. Go live in under 24 hours.

02
🛍️

Customers Discover & Order

Shoppers across Africa and the diaspora find your work through our discovery engine — filtered by style, fabric, occasion, and location. Orders arrive in your dashboard automatically.

03
💰

Fulfil & Get Paid

Ship the order, mark it done, and receive your payout every week. Paystack and Flutterwave handle all the payment complexity — so you focus on designing.

Platform Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

✅

Verified Storefronts

Every designer completes ID & portfolio verification. Customers shop with full confidence knowing every seller is authentic.

🔐

Secure Checkout

Cart, checkout, and payment via Paystack & Flutterwave. Instant email confirmation for buyers and sellers.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

See your best-selling products, peak order days, revenue trends, and customer demographics — all in one place.

🔍

Discovery Engine

Algolia-powered search lets customers filter by style, occasion, fabric type, designer city, and price range.

Order Management

Every order lands in your dashboard with full customer details, size selection, and delivery address. Zero DM management.

Ratings & Reviews

Build social proof that outlasts any Instagram story. Customer reviews are verified, public, and boost your discovery ranking.

Mobile-First Design

Designed for Africa's smartphone-first consumers. Blazing fast on 3G and fully responsive on every device.
